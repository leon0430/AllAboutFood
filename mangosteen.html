Mangosteen

There are exotic food from all over the world. Foods that look pecular and very foreign to the ones we normally see like apples, watermelon and oranges. This page is dedicated to showing people about all the diffrent exotic foods from around the world. First we talk about some exotic food. For example the mangosteen fruit. It is a purple dark fruit with white insides from Southeast-Asia. Its biggest producer is Thailand. It is a very sweet and juicy fruit that has many nutritional benefits. This fruit is as tasteful as it is useful!

Exotic Fruits!

There are exotic food from all over the world. Foods that look pecular and very foreign to the ones we normally see like apples, watermelon and oranges. This page is dedicated to showing people about all the diffrent exotic foods from around the world. First we talk about some exotic food. For example the mangosteen fruit. It is a purple dark fruit with white insides from Southeast-Asia. Its biggest producer is Thailand. It is a very sweet and juicy fruit that has many nutritional benefits. This fruit is as tasteful as it is useful!

Insects?

Southeast Asia is a place full of exotic foods. Ranging from exotic fruits to exotic sea life. In the streets of Thailand one can buy food like crickets You can buy these freid critters on carts or stalls. They are a great representation of Thialands rich cultural and culinary delight.

European Food

Some more appeasing types of foods you can try is a Stroopwafel. They come from the Netherlands. It is made with two very thin crunchy waffles and filled with caramel in the inside. It is one of the mos delicious cookies and is unknown to most of the world. It is meant to be eaten with hot coffee of tea so the steam can melt the caramel. Although they are not very accessible in the US they are not too difficult to make at home. It is a great cookie to make as long as you like tasty treats.

Eat With Caution!

Pufferfish is a known delacacy that is eaten in Japan. They must be eaten with lots of care because they are posionous! The posion in one puffer fish is enough to kill 30 humans and there is no known antidote. It is known as fugu in Japan and only specialized licensed chefts. From 2000 to 2009 there were 23 deaths in 338 fugu poisoning cases.
